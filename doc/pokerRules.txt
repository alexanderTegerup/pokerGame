The poker app simulates No limit Texas holdem.

- If a player make a raise, the raise has to be at least as much as the previous raise in the same round. 

- There is no limit for the maximum raise, a player may bet all his/her chips. 

- There are no limitations of how many times a player may make a raise. 

- At the beginning of the game, the minimum bet is equal to the big blind.
